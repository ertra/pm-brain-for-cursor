## Objective

Complete your first-week onboarding tasks inside the web app so you can start outbound work faster, and let your manager see your progress without asking for status updates.

---

## Applies To

- **Products / packages:** Primary web app (all tiers with rep seats)
- **Roles:** New rep users; managers view team progress (read-only)
- **Prerequisites:** Active rep license; able to sign in to the web app

---

## Before You Begin

- Your admin has assigned you a rep license.
- You can sign in to the web app on a desktop browser (web-only at launch).
- Your team has at least one configured segment so the right task list appears.

---

## Overview

### Summary

The onboarding checklist appears automatically on the web app home for accounts less than 30 days old. It lists the setup tasks new reps should complete in their first two weeks, saves progress as you go, and rolls up team-level completion to your manager.

### End User Experience

After your first login, you land on the app home and see the checklist as a stack of task cards on the right side of the page. Each card has a short description and a **Mark complete** button. As you complete tasks, the card collapses and your progress bar advances. You can sign out and come back on any device — your progress follows you.

Once all P0 tasks are complete, the checklist hides itself; you can re-open it from the **Onboarding** link in the help menu for the rest of the first 30 days.

### Details

- The task list is configured by your admin per customer segment (for example, Enterprise reps see different tasks than Starter reps).
- Completion is tracked at the task level for you and aggregated for your manager (manager sees percentage, not which specific tasks).
- Available in English, German, and French at launch; other locales follow in a later release.

### Setup

#### Open the checklist on first login

1. Sign in to the web app.
2. The checklist appears on the home page automatically — no action needed.
3. If you don't see it, click **Onboarding** in the help menu in the top-right.

#### Complete a task

1. Click a task card to expand it.
2. Follow the in-card instructions (for example, "Connect your mailbox" links you to the integration screen).
3. Click **Mark complete** when you're done. Your progress saves automatically.

#### View your overall progress

1. Open the help menu and click **Onboarding**.
2. The full task list shows everything assigned to you and which items are complete.

## Additional Information

### FAQ

**When does the checklist appear, and to whom?**
Automatically on the web app home for any rep whose account is less than 30 days old. Reps with older accounts can opt in from a banner.

**Can my admin change which tasks I see?**
Yes. Admins configure the task list per segment (Enterprise vs Starter) from the admin console. Changes take effect on the next page load.

**How do managers see my progress?**
Managers see team completion on a dashboard widget and a weekly email digest. The widget shows aggregate completion percentage — not which specific tasks each rep finished.

**Is the checklist available on mobile or in Slack?**
Not at launch. The first release is web-only; mobile and Slack delivery are being evaluated for a future release.

**What happens after I complete all tasks?**
The checklist hides itself from the home page once all P0 tasks are done. You can still reopen it from the **Onboarding** link in the help menu for the remainder of your first 30 days.

---

### Best Practices

- Complete the **Connect your mailbox** task on day one — several later tasks (logging your first customer touchpoint, sending your first sequence) depend on it.
- Treat the checklist as a guide, not a quota. The goal is consistent first-30-day activity, not a perfect score.
- If you're stuck on a task, ask your manager — they can see where the team is getting blocked and use that to update training materials.
