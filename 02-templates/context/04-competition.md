## How PMs should use this file

- Cite **archetypes** unless you have verified, public competitor facts
- Separate **customer-reported** pains from **analyst** or **marketing** claims
- Use **Status quo alternatives** in brief Section B ("how customers solve this today")
- Update `last_updated` and `last_verified` when you refresh win/loss themes

## Implications for PRDs

- Ground competitive parity claims in **Category map** or **Named exemplars** — do not assert feature parity without a source.
- Reference **Win/loss themes** in brief "why now" and competitive pressure.
- Call out **Recent moves** when timing matters for the initiative.
